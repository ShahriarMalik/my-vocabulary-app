Frequently Asked Questions

How does VokabelProfi work?

VokabelProfi offers personalized exercises to help you master German vocabulary with interactive tools and real-time progress tracking. The platform follows a structured learning path based on the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR), ensuring that your learning journey is aligned with standardized language proficiency levels. Best of all, the service is completely free!

Can I track my progress?

Yes, the platform tracks your progress in real-time and provides detailed reports on your learning activities.

What translation services are integrated?

VokabelProfi integrates DeepL API for translations and Google Translation API for audio pronunciation services.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use JWT-based authentication and secure session management to protect your data.

How is the platform themed?

VokabelProfi offers a modern, customizable theme that adapts to both light and dark modes. Users can experience a visually appealing interface that aligns with their personal preferences, ensuring a comfortable and immersive learning environment.

Is VokabelProfi mobile-friendly?

Yes, VokabelProfi is designed to be fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. Whether you're using a phone, tablet, or desktop, the platform adapts seamlessly to provide a smooth and user-friendly experience on all screen sizes.

Is VokabelProfi accessible for disabled users?

Yes, VokabelProfi is designed with accessibility in mind. The platform includes features like screen reader compatibility, keyboard navigation, and appropriate contrast levels to ensure that users with disabilities can effectively engage with the content and improve their language skills.
